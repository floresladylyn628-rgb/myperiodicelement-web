Yttrium is a chemical element, symbolized as Y, and is a transition metal. It's a silvery-metallic element that's relatively stable in air. Yttrium is used in various applications, including phosphors (for fluorescent lights and displays), lasers, superconductors, and specialized ceramics and alloys. Its unique properties make it useful for specific industrial and technological purposes.
